    About

    About Us

    Ibom Business Map is a mobile app platform that connects individuals, businesses & investors to themselves. Our app is a free platform, we are offerring you an adequate visibility for your growth and profit.

    • We offer an interactive, convenient and a quick way to finding Businesses, Customers & finding jobs in Akwa Ibom State.
    • Our solution allows businesses to easily put their businesses on Google Map and also attract investors and customers and vice versa.
    • Analyze NEEDS of registered MSMEs on our platform and matches them to ready investors or financing agencies.
    • Offer real time incentives on Discounts Services, Freebies, Job Vacancies, Utilites, Emergency contacts (security), to engage customers on the App.

    Take a look at our Solutions

    We are giving businesses in Akwa Ibom State visibility on the global stage.

    • 01 Business & Customer Linkage?

      We connect Businesses to Customers who are looking for goods & services in Akwa Ibom State, and customers to business, using Location Based Intelligence.

    • 02 Customer Security Concerns Solved?

      Provide a safe environment for potential customers and clients by listing verified business locations powered by Geographic Information System (GIS).

    • 03 Linkage with potential & available Investors?

      Connect Businesses to Investors who will be interested in investing in Businesses using Data from Business Needs.
